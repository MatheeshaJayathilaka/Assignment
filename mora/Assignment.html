Amazing Places To Visit In Sri Lanka


Sri Lanka is an small island country in the South Asia. It lies in the Indian Ocean, south west of the Bay of Bengal. Sri Lanka has a population of around 22 million in the year 2022. Sri Lanka has a total land area of 65,610 km2. The Main income of Sri lanka is Tourism. Many foreigners and even Sri Lankan's travel in Sri Lanka, because there are various climates in a small area and there are many wonderful places to visit in Sri Lanka. Following are many amazing places in Sri Lanka that you can visit:-

  • Nuwara Eliya
  • Adam's Peak
  • Mirissa Beach
  • Riverston
  • Kithulgala

Nuwara Eliya

❮ ❯

Nuwara Eliya is a city in the tea country hills of central Sri Lanka. The naturally landscaped Hakgala Botanical Gardens displays roses and tree ferns, and shelters monkeys and blue magpies. Nearby Seetha Amman Temple, a colorful Hindu shrine, is decorated with religious figures. Densely forested Galway's Land National Park is a sanctuary for endemic and migratory bird species, including bulbuls and flycatchers.

Adam's Peak

❮ ❯

Adam's Peak or Sri Pada is a tall conical mountain with an elevation of 2,243 m which is located in central Sri lanka. It is a sacred mountain which is worshipped by buddhists, Christians, Muslims and Tamils. Buddhists believe the footprints which are at the top of the mountain are those of Lord Buddha while christians believe that it is the footprint of the Adam. There is a season to climb the Adam's Peak, at that time people are allowed to go to the upper most part of the Sri Padaya. It is a very scenic route sometimes the road is covered with thick mist.

Mirissa Beach

❮ ❯

Mirissa is a small town on the south coast of Sri Lanka, located in the Matara District of the Southern Province. The main attraction is the Mirissa Beach. Mirissa's beach and nightlife make it a popular tourist destination. It is also a fishing port and one of the island's main whale and dolphin watching locations. People can snorkel, scuba dive or even surf in this beach.

Riverston

❮ ❯

Riverston is one of the tourist attraction in Knuckles world heritage area. It also called as Riverston Gap and there is a small mountain to climb. When you reach Transmission tower there is a foot path and after you reach there can see the valley below. It is one of the breathtaking view which I saw in my life. Many miss the opportunity to see it and in the end you can feel the two climate ranges in one area. There is mountain range surrounded by beautiful valley in the gap and beware of your steps.

Kithulgala

❮ ❯

Boasting about adventure sporting, Kithulgala is a town located in West of the road from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya. It's a town located in amid tickly forest hills. It has been once a filming place for the David Lean's 1957 Oscar-winning epic "Bridge on the River Kwai" on the banks of Kelani River. Most visitors for the adventure sporting are from Colombo. The Kitulgala's main attraction is White water rafting where you can ride the tides of Kelani River. Other than that it's popular for sports like confidence jump, Stream Sliding, Waterfall abseiling, Jungle trekking. Bird watching and cave exploration and last but not least river trips and expeditions.
